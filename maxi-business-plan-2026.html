3. CONSULTING METHODOLOGY

The Complete Stack (What Justifies Premium Pricing)

Layer 1: Intake (Client Maturity Diagnostic)

5 conversational questions classify clients Level 0-3: 1. Monetary perspective (speculation vs sound money understanding) 2. Treasury philosophy (preservation, yield, strategic positioning) 3. Risk tolerance reality (actual behavior vs stated) 4. Custody comfort (sovereignty vs intermediary preference) 5. Strategic urgency (early adopter vs wait-and-see)

Result: Know client starting point before advising (no misaligned recommendations)

Layer 2: Framework (What to Deliver)

Three-question model: 1. Should we hold Bitcoin? (Strategic thesis, opportunity cost framing) 2. How much? (Allocation bands: 0.5-2% exploratory, 2-5% strategic, 5-15%+ conviction) 3. How safely? (Custody trade-offs: self vs institutional vs multi-sig)

Layer 3: Process (How to Engage)

6-step consulting flow: 1. Identify motivation (inflation hedge, diversification, signaling, sovereignty) 2. Reframe Bitcoin (monetary infrastructure, not speculation) 3. Allocation logic (risk tolerance, liquidity, time horizon) 4. Custody architecture (sovereignty needs vs regulatory comfort) 5. Execution strategy (OTC desks, DCA vs lump sum, market impact) 6. Governance integration (treasury policy, board approval, key management)

Layer 4: Master Framing (Saylor Mental Model)

Key reframes: - Bitcoin = engineering better treasury system (not investing) - Monetary energy storage (not speculation) - Opportunity cost emphasis (“What’s cost of NOT holding scarce asset?”) - Volatility = short-term noise around long-term adoption - Capital allocation engineering (5 dimensions: scarcity, liquidity, portability, durability, verifiability)

Layer 5: Objection Handling

  • “Too volatile” → “Which risk? Dilution or volatility? Allocation sizing manages both.”
  • “Too risky” → “All treasuries accept risk. Zero allocation = full fiat exposure.”
  • “Too early” → “Waiting is a position. Opportunity cost compounds daily.”
  • “Could lose keys” → “Multisig governance reduces single-point failure.”

Layer 6: Maxi Differentiation (Convergence Lens)

For sophisticated clients (Level 2-3): - “How will AI agents transact economically inside your organization?” - Sustainable Abundance Triad (Bitcoin + AI + Longevity) - “Bitcoin allows AI to become economic actor” (not “AI uses Bitcoin”) - Treasury strategy isn’t just optimization—it’s architecting for AI-native business models

This complete stack is what separates $100/mo generic advice from $2.5K-7.5K/mo strategic advisory.
