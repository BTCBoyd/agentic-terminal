### 12-Session Curriculum (Based on Boyd's Book)

#### **PART 1: Fiat Scarcity & Unsustainable Economy (Sessions 1-2)**

**Session 1: The Fiat Money Problem**
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Understand the history and mechanics of fiat currency debasement
  - Analyze the economic consequences of inflationary monetary policy
  - Recognize the link between unsustainable money and unsustainable cities

- **Pedagogy:**
  - Case Study: Zimbabwe hyperinflation (2008)
  - Simulation: "Print money" exercise (student groups manage fictional central bank)
  - Real-Time Data: M2 money supply charts (US, EU, LatAm)
  - Reflection Journal #1 Prompt: "How does fiat debasement affect your organization's 10-year planning?"

- **Guest Speaker (Optional):** Central bank economist or monetary policy expert

---

**Session 2: Unsustainable Economics & Broken Incentives**
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Connect monetary debasement to environmental/social unsustainability
  - Understand time preference and its impact on business/government decisions
  - Analyze the "cantillon effect" (who benefits from money printing?)

- **Pedagogy:**
  - Case Study: US housing bubble (2008) - malinvestment driven by cheap credit
  - Design Sprint: "Redesign an economic system with sound money" (group exercise)
  - Real-World Examples: Boyd's sustainable cities research (Copenhagen, Barcelona)

- **Assignment:** Read Saifedean Ammous "The Bitcoin Standard" (Chapters 1-3)

---

#### **PART 2: Bitcoinization to Hyperbitcoinization (Sessions 3-7)**

**Session 3: Circular Bitcoin Economies**
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Define "bitcoinization" vs "hyperbitcoinization"
  - Analyze circular economy principles applied to Bitcoin
  - Understand Lightning Network as medium of exchange (not just store of value)

- **Pedagogy:**
  - **Lightning Wallet Immersion (HANDS-ON):**
    - All students download Phoenix or Muun wallet (pre-session setup)
    - Boyd sends each student 10,000 sats (≈$8 at $85K BTC)
    - Students practice: Send/receive, pay Lightning invoice, split bill
  - Case Study: Bitcoin Beach (El Zonte, El Salvador) - full circular economy
  - Simulation: "Build a circular Bitcoin economy" (group design sprint)

- **Guest Speaker:** Bitcoin Beach founder or El Salvador Bitcoin advocate

---

**Session 4: Bitcoin Mining - Energy, Grids, and Sustainability**
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Debunk "Bitcoin wastes energy" myth (energy as feature, not bug)
  - Understand Bitcoin mining's role in renewable energy grid stabilization
  - Analyze mining economics (hash rate, difficulty, profitability)

- **Pedagogy:**
  - Case Study: Texas Bitcoin mining (ERCOT grid stabilization)
  - Real-Time Tools: Hashrate charts (mempool.space), mining profitability calculators
  - Debate: "Is Bitcoin mining an environmental disaster or solution?"
  - Field Trip (if in-person Barcelona cohort): Visit European mining facility

- **Guest Speaker:** Bitcoin miner (operations, economics) or energy grid expert

---

**Session 5: Bitcoin Housing & Real Estate**
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Understand how Bitcoin fixes housing affordability crisis (sound money = lower time preference)
  - Analyze Bitcoin-backed mortgages and real estate transactions
  - Explore tokenization of real estate on Bitcoin (Liquid, RGB protocols)

- **Pedagogy:**
  - Case Study: Housing bubbles (US 2008, China 2020s) - fiat-driven malinvestment
  - Simulation: "Design a Bitcoin-native mortgage product" (group project)
  - Real-World Example: Boyd's sustainable housing research (Barcelona, smart cities)

- **Assignment:** Reflection Journal #2 Prompt: "How would sound money change your industry?"

---

**Session 6: Corporate Bitcoin Treasury Strategy**
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Analyze MicroStrategy's Bitcoin treasury strategy (2020-2026)
  - Understand accounting, tax, and compliance considerations
  - Build a Bitcoin treasury proposal for a corporate balance sheet

- **Pedagogy:**
  - Case Study: MicroStrategy (Michael Saylor's strategy, results, risks)
  - Case Study: Tesla's Bitcoin experiment (bought, sold, lessons learned)
  - Spreadsheet Exercise: "Build a 5% Bitcoin allocation model" (Excel/Google Sheets)
  - Debate: "Should every company hold Bitcoin on their balance sheet?"

- **Guest Speaker:** CFO or Treasurer from Bitcoin-forward company (if accessible)

---

**Session 7: Bitcoin Scaling - Lightning, Liquid, Layers**
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Understand Bitcoin's layered architecture (base layer, Lightning, Liquid, sidechains)
  - Analyze Lightning Network's role in payments scaling (instant, low-cost)
  - Explore emerging protocols (RGB, Taro) for assets on Bitcoin

- **Pedagogy:**
  - **Lightning Deep Dive (HANDS-ON):**
    - Students open Lightning channels, route payments
    - Explore Lightning apps (Stacker News, THNDR games, LN Markets)
  - Case Study: Lightning Network adoption (2021-2026 growth metrics)
  - Technical Demo: How a Lightning payment works (routing, HTLCs)

- **Guest Speaker:** Lightning Network developer or infrastructure provider (e.g., Lightning Labs, Breez)

---

#### **PART 3: Bitcoin Singularity & New Economic Order (Sessions 8-12)**

**Session 8: Bitcoin, Food Systems, and Regenerative Agriculture**
- **Learning Objectives:**
  - Connect Bitcoin's low time preference to sustainable food production
