## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Event Conversion
**Mitigation:** Pre-event pipeline building ensures we're not dependent solely on Mita. If event underperforms, we still have 50-100 qualified leads from other channels.

### Risk 2: Bitcoin Price Recovery Makes Product Less Attractive
**Paradox:** If Bitcoin pumps back to $100K+, real estate loan becomes "worse deal"
**Mitigation:** 
- Reframe messaging: "Lock in gains - use property to preserve Bitcoin stack"
- Shift to wealth preservation vs accumulation narrative
- Pivot to custody/wealth management products

### Risk 3: Regulatory Changes
**Mexico's ASOFOM framework could change**
**Mitigation:** ArcadiaB's regulatory moat (already certified) means we adapt faster than competitors. Stay close to CNBV updates.

### Risk 4: Competitor Entry
**Other players could copy model**
**Mitigation:** First-mover advantage + Mita credibility + thought leadership = brand moat. Speed matters. Close 5-10 deals before copycats emerge.
