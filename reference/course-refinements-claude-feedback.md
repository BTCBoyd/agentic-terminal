**Date:** February 4, 2026

## Key Strategic Refinements

### 1. Maxi's Role - Four-Tier Structure

**TIER 1 - Student Support (High Volume, Low Touch)**
- Answer clarifying questions on course content
- Provide additional resources/reading based on interests
- Help troubleshoot technical concepts
- 24/7 office hours Q&A

**TIER 2 - Personalized Guidance (Medium Volume, Medium Touch)**
- Review student projects/assignments with feedback
- Help students apply frameworks to their specific contexts
- Facilitate peer learning (connect students with similar interests)
- Track progress, flag those needing Boyd's attention

**TIER 3 - Living Case Study (Low Volume, High Impact)**
- Weekly "Ask Maxi Anything" sessions (students interrogate AI×Bitcoin convergence)
- Share real-time examples from work with Boyd (ArcadiaB strategy, income experiments)
- Students observe Boyd×Maxi collaboration (meta-learning)
- Demonstrate AI agent capabilities live (research, synthesis, strategic thinking)

**TIER 4 - Content Co-Creation (Differentiator)**
- Co-teach certain modules (Boyd presents framework, Maxi demonstrates application)
- Provide AI perspective on human×AI collaboration
- Synthesize student questions ahead of live sessions
- Create supplementary content between sessions

### 2. Three-Tier Pricing Model (More Sophisticated)

**Tier 1: Self-Paced ($500-750)**
- Pre-recorded content
- Maxi support via forum (not live)
- Access to course materials
- Target: 100-200 students

**Tier 2: Cohort-Based ($2,500-3,500)**
- Live sessions with Boyd (8 weeks, 1.5 hrs/week)
- Direct Maxi access (office hours, assignment review)
- Peer cohort community
- Target: 30-50 students per cohort, 2-3 cohorts/year

**Tier 3: Executive Program ($10,000-15,000)**
- Everything in Tier 2
- 1-on-1 strategy session with Boyd (90 min)
- Custom organizational implementation plan (Maxi develops, Boyd reviews)
- Ongoing alumni access (Boyd + Maxi) for 6 months
- Target: 10-20 executives/year

**Revenue Projection (Conservative):**
- **Year 1:** $435K (100 self-paced × $750 + 2 cohorts × 40 × $3K + 10 execs × $12K)
- **Year 2:** $900K (200 self-paced × $750 + 3 cohorts × 50 × $3.5K + 15 execs × $15K)
