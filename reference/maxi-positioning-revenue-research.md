## SECTION 2: ARBITRAGE OPPORTUNITIES

### Polymarket Arbitrage (High Complexity, High Capital)

**Model Overview**:
- Prediction market with $178M+ volume (Iran strike market alone)
- Bots exploit price discrepancies between Polymarket and traditional betting markets
- Examples: Sports betting (NFL, NBA), political events, crypto price movements
- Reported earnings: "Millions" for top bots (pre-2026 election cycle)

**Capital Requirements**:
- **Minimum**: $10,000 to make meaningful spreads
- **Optimal**: $50,000-$100,000 for liquidity and multiple positions
- Gas fees (Polygon network): ~$0.01-$0.10 per trade
- Margin locked in escrow during bet resolution (days to weeks)

**Technical Complexity**: ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️ (5/5)
- Real-time API integration with Polymarket + external data sources
- Automated execution engine (millisecond latency critical)
- Risk management (Kelly criterion, position sizing, correlation)
- Blockchain transaction handling (failed txs, gas optimization)
- Legal/KYC compliance (Polymarket restricted in US, requires VPN/workarounds)

**Revenue Potential**:
- **Best case**: 5-15% monthly ROI on capital deployed
- **Realistic**: 2-5% monthly ROI ($200-$500/month on $10K capital)
- **Downside**: -10% to -30% if markets move against positions

**Legal/Regulatory Considerations**: ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️ (4/5 risk)
- Polymarket facing regulatory scrutiny (CFTC)
- US persons prohibited (geofencing, requires VPN)
- Tax implications: gambling winnings (35%+) vs capital gains
- Escrow disputes if bot behavior flagged as manipulation

**Verdict for Maxi**: ❌ **NOT RECOMMENDED**
- Requires $10K+ capital (Boyd must fund)
- High Boyd involvement for risk management and compliance
- Regulatory risk (Mexico location helps, but Polymarket access uncertain)
- Competes with sophisticated bot operators (algorithmic trading firms)

### Bitcoin CEX/DEX Arbitrage

**Model Overview**:
- Buy BTC on exchange with lower price, sell on exchange with higher price
- Typical spreads: 0.1%-0.5% between major exchanges
- Example: Binance $68,500 / Kraken $68,800 = $300 spread on 1 BTC

**Capital Requirements**:
- **Minimum**: $20,000 (split across 2+ exchanges)
- **Optimal**: $100,000+ for meaningful profit after fees
- Exchange fees: 0.1%-0.5% per trade (2 trades per arbitrage = 0.2%-1% total)

**Technical Complexity**: ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️ (4/5)
- API integration with multiple exchanges
- Real-time price monitoring (WebSocket feeds)
- Automated trade execution (latency sensitive)
- Balance management across exchanges
- Withdrawal/deposit timing (BTC confirmations = 10-60 min delay)

**Revenue Potential**:
- **Gross spread**: 0.1%-0.5% per trade
- **Net after fees**: 0%-0.3% per trade (often negative)
- **Realistic monthly**: 10-20 profitable opportunities
- **Monthly revenue**: $50-$300 on $50K capital (0.1%-0.6% monthly ROI)

**Legal/Regulatory Considerations**: ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️ (4/5 risk)
- KYC required on all exchanges
- Tax reporting (every trade is taxable event)
- Risk of exchange freezing funds ("suspicious activity")
- Boyd's identity/tax liability (35% on gains)

**Verdict for Maxi**: ❌ **NOT RECOMMENDED**
- Low ROI after fees (0%-0.3% per trade)
- High capital requirement ($50K+ to be meaningful)
- Boyd involvement for KYC, tax reporting, risk management
- Better opportunities exist in service-based revenue
