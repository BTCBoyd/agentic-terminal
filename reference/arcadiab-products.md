## Core Product Suite

### Bitcoin-Backed Lending

**Product Description:**
Collateralized loans using Bitcoin as security, enabling holders to access liquidity in pesos or dollars without selling their Bitcoin. Fully compliant with Mexican ASOFOM regulations.

**Key Features:**
- **Loan-to-Value (LTV)**: Typically 50% LTV ($10,000 BTC collateral = $5,000 loan)
- **Currency options**: Loans in Mexican pesos (MXN) or US dollars (USD)
- **Interest rates**: Competitive 8-12% APR depending on LTV and term
- **Loan terms**: Flexible 6 months to 3 years
- **Custody**: Institutional-grade multi-signature cold storage with insurance
- **No credit checks**: Bitcoin collateral eliminates traditional creditworthiness requirements
- **Margin calls**: Clear LTV thresholds with ability to add collateral or pay down principal

**Target Customers:**
- Individual Bitcoin holders needing liquidity for life events, business opportunities, emergencies
- Entrepreneurs accessing working capital without selling Bitcoin treasury
- Real estate investors using Bitcoin for bridge financing
- High-net-worth individuals for sophisticated wealth preservation

**StoryBrand Messaging:**
*"Don't sell your Bitcoin. Borrow against it."* - Your Bitcoin is your most valuable long-term asset. With ArcadiaB's Bitcoin-backed loans, you access the pesos or dollars you need today while keeping 100% of your future Bitcoin upside.
