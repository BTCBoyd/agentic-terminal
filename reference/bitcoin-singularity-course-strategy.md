## PART 3: CURRICULUM DESIGN

### Course Structure: 8-Week Bitcoin Singularity Cohort

**Course Title:** *The Bitcoin Singularity: Navigating the Convergence of AI, Energy, and Monetary Innovation*

**Subtitle:** An executive framework for understanding and capitalizing on the Sustainable Abundance Triad

**Target Audience:**
1. **Corporate Innovation Teams**: VPs of Strategy, Innovation Directors at Fortune 1000
2. **Family Office Analysts**: Investment committees managing $50M+ portfolios
3. **LatAm Bitcoin Entrepreneurs**: Founders building on Bitcoin rails in emerging markets
4. **Institutional Allocators**: Pension funds, endowments exploring Bitcoin treasury strategies

**Prerequisites:**
- No technical background required
- Basic understanding of Bitcoin helpful but not required
- Executive-level strategic thinking (director+ or founder experience)

**Format:**
- 8 weekly 90-minute live sessions (Wednesdays 12pm ET / 6pm CET)
- Pre-recorded video lectures (30-45min/week)

- Final capstone: Bitcoin×AI strategic memo for your organization

**Cohort Size:** 15-25 participants (Maven recommendation for intimacy + economics)
