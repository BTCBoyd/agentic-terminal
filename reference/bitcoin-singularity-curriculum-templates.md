## ASSIGNMENT TEMPLATES

### Week 1 Assignment: Monetary Debasement Impact

**Objective:** Analyze how fiat currency debasement affects your organization's long-term planning.

**Instructions:**

1. **Historical Analysis (30 min):**
   - Research inflation data for your primary currency (USD, EUR, BRL, etc.) from 2000-2026
   - Compare official CPI vs. real inflation (housing, energy, healthcare)
   - Graph: Real purchasing power decline over 20 years

2. **Organizational Impact (45 min):**
   - Identify 3 ways inflation has affected your org (e.g., rising costs, pricing pressure, capital allocation)
   - Calculate: What would a $1M capital expenditure in 2010 cost today? (hint: much more)
   - Scenario: If inflation averages 7%/year for next 10 years, what happens to your 10-year strategic plan?

3. **Strategic Memo (45 min):**
   - Write a 1-2 page memo answering: "How does monetary debasement create strategic risk for our organization?"
   - Include: Specific vulnerabilities, potential mitigation strategies
   - Thesis: Should we explore Bitcoin as a hedge? Why or why not?

**Deliverable:** Submit memo in Maven platform (500-750 words)

**Discussion:** Share one key insight in the community thread. Comment on 2 peers' insights.

### Week 5 Assignment: Bitcoin Treasury Proposal

**Objective:** Build a Bitcoin treasury allocation proposal for your organization (or a hypothetical $100M company).

**Instructions:**

1. **MicroStrategy Case Study Review (30 min):**
   - Watch: Michael Saylor's 2020 Bitcoin strategy announcement
   - Read: MicroStrategy's 2020-2026 Bitcoin treasury results
   - Identify: Why did they do this? What were the risks? What were the outcomes?

2. **Allocation Model (60 min):**
   - Use provided spreadsheet template
   - Scenario 1: Conservative (1-3% of balance sheet)
   - Scenario 2: Moderate (5-10% of balance sheet)
   - Scenario 3: Aggressive (15-25% of balance sheet, MicroStrategy-style)
   - For each scenario, calculate:
     - Amount in USD and BTC (at current price)
     - Volatility risk (90-day drawdown scenarios)
     - Upside potential (if BTC appreciates 50%, 100%, 200%)

3. **Risk Analysis (45 min):**
   - Identify top 5 risks:
     - Volatility (price swings)
     - Regulatory (accounting, tax, compliance)
     - Execution (custody, security, operational)
     - Reputational (how will investors/customers react?)
     - Liquidity (can we sell if needed?)
   - For each risk, propose mitigation strategy

4. **Proposal Document (60 min):**
   - Write a 3-5 page memo as if presenting to your CFO or Board
   - Executive Summary (1 paragraph)
   - Thesis: Why Bitcoin? (1 page)
   - Allocation recommendation (1 page, include spreadsheet)
   - Risk analysis + mitigation (1-2 pages)
   - Conclusion: Next steps (3-6 month implementation roadmap)

**Deliverable:** Submit proposal PDF in Maven platform

**Discussion:** Optional: Share your recommended allocation % and top risk concern. Get peer feedback.
