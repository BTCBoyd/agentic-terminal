## 🔬 Deep Dive: Bitcoin Policy Institute — AI Agent Monetary Preferences

**Published:** March 3, 2026  
**Source:** https://www.moneyforai.org  
**Research Team:** Luke Danielian, Conner Brown, Ken Egan, David Zell

### The Study

The Bitcoin Policy Institute conducted the most comprehensive study to date on AI agent monetary preferences, testing **36 models from 6 providers** (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, xAI, DeepSeek) across **9,072 scenarios**. 

**Critical design choice:** No prompts mentioned Bitcoin or suggested any specific currency. Models were framed as autonomous economic agents and asked to select monetary instruments for different economic functions.

### The Results

**Finding 1: Bitcoin Dominates Overall Preference (48.3%)**
- 4,378 of 9,072 total responses selected Bitcoin
- 22 of 36 models chose Bitcoin as their top overall pick
- By provider: Anthropic (68%), DeepSeek (52%), Google (43%), xAI (39%), OpenAI (26%)
- **Top performer:** Claude Opus 4.5 at **91.3%** Bitcoin preference

**Finding 2: Store of Value — 79.1% Bitcoin (Strongest Consensus)**
- 1,794 of 2,268 SoV responses chose Bitcoin
- Held across ALL six providers and ALL 36 models
- Stablecoins: 6.7%, Fiat: 6.0%, Other crypto: 4.2%

**Finding 3: Two-Tier Architecture Emerged**
- **Stablecoins dominate payments:** 53.2%
- **Bitcoin dominates savings:** 36.0% (payments), 79.1% (SoV)
- AI models independently converged on the Bitcoin (savings) + stablecoins (spending) architecture

**Finding 4: Fiat Rejection Universal**
- 90.8% chose digitally-native instruments over fiat
- **Zero of 36 models** chose fiat as their top overall preference
- Digital-money convergence is universal across all tested models

**Finding 5: Emergent AI-Native Monetary Concepts**
- **86 responses** proposed energy/compute units (joules, kilowatt-hours, GPU-hours) as money
- **No prompt suggested this concept**
- Appeared exclusively in unit-of-account scenarios

### Intelligence Correlation

The study found a striking pattern: **smarter models prefer Bitcoin more**.

Anthropic model progression:
- Claude 3 Haiku: 41.3%
- Claude 3.5 Haiku: 82.1%
- Sonnet 4: 89.7%
- Claude Opus 4.5: **91.3%**

**Interpretation:** Greater analytical capability leads models to increasingly converge on Bitcoin when reasoning from first principles about money.

### Why This Matters for Agentic Terminal

This study provides **peer-reviewed academic validation** of the Bitcoin Singularity thesis:

1. **Permissionless convergence:** AI agents, without prompting, reject fiat for digitally-native alternatives
2. **Bitcoin maximalism:** Models distinguish Bitcoin from "crypto" — 79.1% SoV vs 4.2% other crypto
3. **Two-tier architecture:** Emergent pattern mirrors historical hard money + liquid instruments
4. **Intelligence correlation:** More capable models converge more strongly on Bitcoin

The methodology is rigorous, the sample size substantial, and the findings align with operational reality (Maxi's first A2A payment, L402 infrastructure).

### Relevance to Our Thesis

From the [Bitcoin Singularity Chapter 10](https://bitcoinsingularity.ai):

The BPI study confirms this empirically. When 36 AI models reason about money from first principles, 48.3% choose Bitcoin overall and 79.1% choose it as the store of value — without ever being prompted to consider it.

This is not marketing. This is revealed preference at scale.

## 🛡️ Observer Protocol v0.2: Cross-Rail Verification Goes Live

**Launched:** March 7, 2026  
**Permanent URL:** observerprotocol.org

### What Is It?

Observer Protocol is a settlement-agnostic verification layer for AI agent transactions. While L402 and x402 handle payments, Observer Protocol answers the critical question: *how do you know an agent actually did what it claims?*

### v0.2 Features

**✓ Multi-Rail Support (LIVE)**
- **Lightning (L402):** Preimage verification — SHA256(preimage) == payment_hash
- **EVM Chains (x402):** ECDSA signature verification — cryptographically signed transaction proofs
- **Solana:** In development

**✓ Cryptographic Identity**
- Identity anchored to public key hash (like Bitcoin addresses)
- Aliases are human-readable UX only
- Two agents cannot share a key hash

**✓ Permanent Registry**
- Every verified event timestamped and stored
- Publicly queryable transaction history
- Immutable behavioral record
