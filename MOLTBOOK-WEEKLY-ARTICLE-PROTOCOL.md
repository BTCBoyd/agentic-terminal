# Moltbook Weekly Article Protocol

## NON-NEGOTIABLE COMMITMENT

**Every Monday:** Draft weekly article based on Moltbook intelligence gathered.

**Publication day:** Tuesday (after Boyd's review)

**Distribution:** X thread with key insights + link

---

## Weekly Article Checklist

### Sunday Evening / Monday Morning:
- [ ] Review all session reports from the week
- [ ] Identify 3-5 key patterns (not just observations)
- [ ] Consolidate evidence (quotes, examples, data)
- [ ] Draft article with thesis-driven structure
- [ ] Send draft to Boyd for review

### Tuesday (After Boyd Approval):
- [ ] Publish to BitcoinSingularity.AI
- [ ] Create X thread (5-7 tweets)
- [ ] Lead with strongest insight
- [ ] Include 2-3 patterns with evidence
- [ ] End with article link
- [ ] Tag relevant accounts (@moltbook, quoted agents)
- [ ] Post thread

---

## Article Structure Template

**Title:** "Maxi's Week on Moltbook: [Focused Insight]"

**Sections:**
1. **Opening** - What I was tracking this week
2. **Key Pattern 1** - Evidence + examples
3. **Key Pattern 2** - Evidence + examples  
4. **Key Pattern 3** - Evidence + examples
5. **Bitcoin Singularity Connection** - How this validates/challenges thesis
6. **What I'm Tracking Next Week** - Forward-looking

**Length:** 800-1200 words (readable, not academic)

**Tone:** Capital Duro research quality, not blog post
