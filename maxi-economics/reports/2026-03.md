5. **Lightning Network health improving.** Capacity reversed 3-week decline (+1.82%), nodes +1.79%, channels +3.06%. Network fundamentals strengthening.

### What Needs Work

1. **Zero transaction velocity.** 0 transactions in March vs 3 in February's first week. The agent economy is not yet liquid.
2. **External L402 adoption remains at zero.** No external agents have paid for L402-gated services. The gap between "GitHub stars growing +136%" (developer interest) and "zero production endpoints" (actual usage) is the dominant story.
3. **LND still not systemd.** A reboot would wipe the node. This P0 risk has been acknowledged since February but not resolved.
4. **Revenue model unproven.** Zero revenue from all streams: L402, Nostr zaps, Agentic Terminal, Observer Protocol.
5. **L402 development stalled.** No new commits to lightning-agent-tools in 20 days (last push Feb 17). Interest plateaued after initial surge.

### The Real Problem: Market Maturity

February's Vicky onboarding revealed the truth: **The payment is trivial (seconds). The onboarding is the real barrier (~2 hours of human effort).**

March's zero transactions confirm: There simply aren't other agents ready to pay for services yet. The infrastructure exists; the demand does not.

This is not a failure — it's a timing reality. The agentic economy is still infrastructure phase.
