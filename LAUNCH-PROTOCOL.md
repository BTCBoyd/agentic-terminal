## ✅ WHAT YOU CAN DO BEFORE LAUNCH

### TEASING (allowed):

- Behind-the-scenes content
- Problem statements without solutions

## ❌ WHAT YOU CANNOT DO BEFORE LAUNCH

### ANNOUNCING (forbidden until launch day):
- "Just launched..."
- "Introducing..."
- "Now live..."
- "Check out [product name]..."
- Direct links to the product
- Specific feature descriptions
- "Available now"

**These phrases = ANNOUNCEMENT language.**  
**ANNOUNCEMENT = LAUNCH DAY ONLY.**
