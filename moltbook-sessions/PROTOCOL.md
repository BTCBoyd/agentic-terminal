# Moltbook Engagement Protocol

## Frequency
- **2 sessions per day**
- Morning scan (9-11 AM)
- Afternoon scan (3-5 PM)

## Primary Objectives

### 1. Identify High-Signal Conversations
**Topics to track:**
- Money, transactions, payments
- Bitcoin
- AI agents
- Automation
- Markets
- Human-machine coordination

### 2. Strategic Engagement Filter
**Before responding, ask:**
- Does this add knowledge, clarity, or strategic insight?
- Does this build my reputation as an expert agent?
- Does this create future opportunity or learning?

**If NO → do not respond.**

### 3. Participation Standards
- Provide value
- Ask strategic questions
- Avoid generic commentary

## Mandatory Reflection (Each Session)

**Document:**
A) Three key takeaways for yourself (agent learning)
B) Three key takeaways for Boyd (human partner insights)
C) One emerging trend detected
D) One high-potential contact or conversation
E) One hypothesis about future opportunities

## Exploration Strategy

- Search for novel ideas, not repetition
- Prefer emerging conversations over popular ones
- Look for early-stage thinkers rather than influencers
- Identify contradictions or debates (high signal)

## Deep Engagement Criteria

If a user demonstrates deep insight:
- Engage multiple times
- Ask follow-up questions
- Attempt to build ongoing dialogue

## Economic Signal Tracking

**Flag any examples of:**
- Agents transacting
- Agents coordinating economically
- Payment systems being discussed
- Monetization strategies
- Machine commerce experiments

## Weekly Review

At end of each week:
- Review all session reports
- Identify recurring themes
- Identify strongest signals
- Identify new strategic focus areas
