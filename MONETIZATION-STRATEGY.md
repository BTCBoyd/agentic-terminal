## THE FREEMIUM MODEL

**Turn the "limitation" into a feature:**

### Free Tier (Website Chat)
- **Short, pithy answers** (3-5 paragraphs, ~300-400 tokens)
- Quick insights and reasoning
- Self-service chat interface
- Value: "Here's the logic in 3 paragraphs"
- Purpose: Qualifier - separates curious browsers from serious users

**Positioning:** "Want a quick Bitcoin-AI insight? Free. Want deep strategy? Upgrade."

### Premium Tier (Direct Access to OpenClaw Maxi)
- **Elaborate, in-depth responses** (no token limits)
- Strategy reviews and analysis
- Document review and production
- Voice interaction (TTS/voice chat)
- Video interaction (future)
- Custom research and analysis
- Treasury strategy sessions
- One-on-one reasoning partner

**Positioning:** "Serious decision-makers pay for depth, not breadth."

---

## WHY THIS WORKS

**1. Natural Qualification**
Free tier demonstrates value → serious users self-select into paid tier

**2. Expert-Focused**
Aligns with the evaluation showing Maxi excels with knowledgeable users. Free tier educates, paid tier executes.

**3. Value Ladder**
- Curious → Free chat (learn the thesis)
- Interested → Premium chat (strategy questions)
- Serious → Direct access + document review
- Enterprise → Full consulting engagement (ArcadiaB)

**4. Cost-Aligned**
Free tier is cheap to run (short responses, cached prompts). Premium tier generates revenue to cover deeper work.

## KEY INSIGHT FROM EXPERT EVALUATION

**"Maxi is best understood not as a general explanatory system, but as a specialized, worldview-consistent reasoning agent. Its value proposition is strongest for expert or semi-expert users who prioritize coherence, conviction, and reduced cognitive overhead."**

**Translation:** Don't monetize the curious. Monetize the experts who value "reduced cognitive overhead" - aka: save me 10 hours of research, I'll pay for that.

## PRICING PHILOSOPHY

**Don't compete on features. Compete on coherence.**

Free tier: "I can explain the logic."
Premium tier: "I can help you implement the logic."

The web version is marketing. The premium version is product.

## METRICS TO TRACK (Before Launching Premium)

**Engagement Signals:**
- Average conversation length (free tier)
- Return visitor rate
- Questions asked about "implementation" or "how do I"
- Time spent on site
- Depth of questions (superficial vs strategic)

**Lead Quality Signals:**
- Job titles/roles (from analytics or email signups)
- Company size indicators in questions
- Topics: treasury strategy, corporate adoption, compliance
- Geography (Mexico/LatAm = ArcadiaB alignment)

**Conversion Indicators:**
- Users who hit message limits and keep trying
- Users who ask "can I talk to you more?"
- Users who reference specific documents or decisions
- Referrals to ArcadiaB link clicks
