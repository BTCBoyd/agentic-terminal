> "The bottleneck for the agent economy is shifting from intelligence

**Preferred narrative for newsletter/investors:**

- KYA is the *outcome*. Observer Protocol is the *infrastructure*.
- Identity attestation (`agent_principal`, `capability_hash`) is v0.2
