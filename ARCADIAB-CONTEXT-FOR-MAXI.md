**Bitcoin-Backed Loans (B2X):**
- Individuals/companies hold Bitcoin
- Need fiat liquidity for expenses
- Borrow against BTC collateral
- Keep Bitcoin exposure, get spending power

**Real Estate + Bitcoin Leverage (NEW - Launching):**
- Client owns real estate but wants Bitcoin exposure
- Transfer property title into ArcadiaB's ASOFOM fiduciary trust
- ArcadiaB generates fiat loan secured by property
