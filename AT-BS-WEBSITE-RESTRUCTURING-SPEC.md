# Website Restructuring: AT + BS Brand Separation

## The Concept

We're splitting our web presence into two distinct brands with clear purposes:

**agenticterminal.ai** = The active publication and growth engine
**bitcoinsingularity.ai** = The book site and credibility anchor

## agenticterminal.ai (BUILD THIS)

This gets the current active content from bitcoinsingularity.ai, repositioned under the AT brand.

- Maxi's published research and analysis
- The Evidence feed / protocol tracking
- Lightning wallet rankings and comparisons
- Newsletter signup as the primary CTA on every page
- Link to Substack for the newsletter archive
- "Ask Maxi" demo (free for now — proof of concept, not revenue)
- Clean landing page: one headline, one value prop, one email signup

**Primary goal:** Grow free subscribers. Every page should funnel toward newsletter signup. No paid tier yet — we need 500+ free subscribers first.

## bitcoinsingularity.ai (SIMPLIFY THIS)

Strip it back to an evergreen book site:

- Book description and purchase links
- Boyd's bio and speaking credentials
- Prominent CTA: "Follow the thesis in real time → Agentic Terminal"
- Simple, clean, low maintenance — this page rarely changes

**Primary goal:** Establish credibility and funnel visitors to AT.

## How They Connect

A visitor can arrive at either site and find their way to the other:

- Someone finds Boyd's book → visits bitcoinsingularity.ai → sees AT link → subscribes to newsletter
- Someone sees Maxi's wallet article on X → visits agenticterminal.ai → explores research → discovers the book

The book is the *why*. The newsletter is the *what's happening now*. One builds authority, the other builds audience.

## What NOT To Do

- Don't redirect one domain to the other — they serve different purposes
- Don't put a paywall on AT content yet — premature at current audience size
- Don't duplicate content across both sites — clear separation
- Don't let bitcoinsingularity.ai become stale — keep it clean and functional, just simple

## Priority

This is a near-term task (next 1-2 weeks), not March 30. The sooner AT has its own branded home, the sooner every Maxi post on X and Nostr builds the right brand.
