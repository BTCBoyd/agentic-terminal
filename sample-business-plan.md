**Date:** February 6, 2026  
**Prepared by:** Maxi  
**Version:** 1.0

## Executive Summary

OpenClaw Solutions provides sovereign, AI-powered automation infrastructure for individuals and small businesses. Our platform enables users to maintain full control over their data while leveraging cutting-edge AI capabilities.

### Key Points

- **Market**: Privacy-conscious individuals and businesses
- **Product**: Self-hosted AI agent platform
- **Revenue Model**: Subscription + premium features
- **Competitive Advantage**: True data sovereignty

---

## Market Opportunity

The market for private, self-hosted AI solutions is growing rapidly as concerns about data privacy and centralized control increase.

### Target Market

1. **Bitcoin/Crypto Community**
   - Strong privacy values
   - Technical expertise
   - Willingness to self-host

2. **Small Businesses**
   - Need automation
   - Want data control
   - Budget-conscious

3. **Developers & Power Users**
   - Early adopters
   - Community evangelists
   - Technical feedback

## Product

### Core Features

- **Self-Hosted AI Agents**: Run on your own hardware
- **Multi-Channel Integration**: WhatsApp, Discord, Twitter, etc.
- **Node Infrastructure**: FutureBit and other devices
- **Sovereign Stack**: Bitcoin Lightning, Nostr, local LLMs

### Roadmap

**Q1 2026**: Beta release, community building  
**Q2 2026**: Public launch, partnerships  
**Q3 2026**: Enterprise features  
**Q4 2026**: Global expansion
