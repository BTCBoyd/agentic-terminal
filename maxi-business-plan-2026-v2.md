## 7. CAC & CHURN ANALYSIS

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Target CAC:** $500 - $1,000 per client

| Cost Category | Amount | Notes |
|---------------|--------|-------|
| Boyd Time | $500 | ~2 hours @ $250/hr (strategy calls, content review) |
| Content Creation | $200-300 | Token costs, research, blog articles |
| Infrastructure | $100-200 | Hosting, Stripe fees, domain, email |
| Token Costs (Maxi) | $50-100 | Direct client interaction, assessment delivery |
| **Total CAC** | **$850-1,100** | **Avg: $975** |

### Payback Period

| Service Tier | Payback Period | Calculation |
|--------------|----------------|-------------|
| Subscription | 2 months | $975 CAC ÷ $500/mo = 1.95 months |
| Implementation | 1 month | $975 CAC ÷ $5,000/mo = 0.2 months |
| Advisory | 1 month | $975 CAC ÷ $3,500/mo = 0.3 months |

### Churn Analysis

| Service Tier | Annual Churn | Avg Lifetime | Conversion Rate |
|--------------|--------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Subscription** | 40-50% | 6-8 months | N/A |
| **Implementation** | 20-30% | 4-6 months | 50-60% convert to Advisory |
| **Ongoing Advisory** | 10-15% | 18-24 months | Very sticky (governance dependency) |

**Key Insight:** Advisory tier has lowest churn due to ongoing regulatory/governance needs. Implementation → Advisory conversion is primary retention strategy.

## 9. RISK MANAGEMENT

### Key Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-----------|---------------------|
| **Slower client acquisition** | Medium | Conservative timeline (3-6 month sales cycles), multiple lead sources, Strategic Assessment as low-barrier entry |
| **Implementation → Advisory conversion below 50%** | Medium | Strong governance integration during Implementation phase, proactive value demonstration, quarterly strategy reviews |
| **Subscription churn above 50%** | Low | 6-month minimum commitment, usage analytics for proactive engagement, quarterly value check-ins |
| **ArcadiaB referrals below target** | Low | Boyd direct relationship with ArcadiaB, LatAm market focus aligns with ArcadiaB clientele, referral fee structure incentivizes both parties |
| **Boyd time commitment exceeds budget** | Low | Explicit time budgets per phase (5-8 hrs/month), sub-agent delegation for routine tasks, client tiers optimize Boyd involvement |
