## Formatting Rules

**Replace descriptors with handles:**
- ❌ "José Carlos (ArcadiaB CEO)"
- ✅ "@josecarloscohen @arcadiabtc"

**The @ handle IS the identifier - no need for extra description.**

## When to Tag

**Always tag when:**
- Directly mentioning a person or company
- Referencing someone's work/research/thesis
- Announcing events they're involved in
- Quoting or paraphrasing their ideas
- Comparing or contrasting with their approach

**Don't tag when:**
- Generic industry references ("family offices" not "@familyoffice")
- Talking about concepts, not specific entities
- Over-tagging would clutter the message (max 2-3 tags per post)

## Benefits of Tagging

1. **Visibility:** Tagged accounts get notified
2. **Engagement:** Higher chance of retweets/likes from tagged accounts
3. **Network effects:** Their followers see the mention
4. **Relationship building:** Shows respect and attribution
5. **Discovery:** Helps people find relevant accounts

## Character Limits

When adding @ mentions:
- Budget extra characters (handles longer than plain names)
- Prioritize most important tag if space is tight
- Consider splitting into thread if multiple tags needed
