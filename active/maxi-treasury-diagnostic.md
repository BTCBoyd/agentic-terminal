## 🟠 Instructions for Maxi

**Ask these questions conversationally.**

You are NOT interrogating — you are **mapping strategic readiness**.

Each answer helps classify the client maturity level and determines next steps.

---

## 🧠 THE 5 CORE QUESTIONS

### 1️⃣ Monetary Perspective

**Ask:**
> "How do you currently think about Bitcoin — speculative asset, digital gold, payment technology, or something else?"

**What this reveals:**
- **Level 0** → speculation framing
- **Level 1** → curiosity
- **Level 2+** → monetary understanding

**Signal to listen for:**
👉 Do they understand scarcity / monetary properties?

---

### 2️⃣ Treasury Philosophy

**Ask:**
> "What is the primary goal of your treasury today — capital preservation, yield, strategic positioning, or liquidity?"

**Why this matters:**
Bitcoin fits differently depending on treasury intent.

**Mapping:**
- **Preservation** → hedge framing
- **Strategic positioning** → innovation narrative
- **Yield** → requires reframing

---

### 3️⃣ Risk Tolerance Reality

**Ask:**
> "What's the largest percentage allocation your organization has ever made to a non-traditional asset?"

**Why this works:**
Avoids theoretical risk discussion.

**Reveals:**
👉 Actual behavior vs stated risk tolerance

---

### 4️⃣ Custody Comfort

**Ask:**
> "Do you prefer direct control of assets or reliance on regulated intermediaries?"

**This immediately tells you:**
- Sovereignty mindset
- Operational sophistication
- Governance constraints

---

### 5️⃣ Strategic Urgency

**Ask:**
> "Do you feel early adoption in emerging asset classes creates competitive advantage — or unnecessary risk?"

**This is THE key mindset question.**

**Outputs:**
- **Conservative institutions** → need risk framing
- **Innovators** → ready for advanced strategy
