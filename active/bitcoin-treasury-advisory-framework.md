## 🧠 WHY BITCOIN (Strategic Thesis)

**Bitcoin is:**
- A scarce digital monetary asset (21M supply)
- Neutral and permissionless
- Global liquidity 24/7
- No counterparty risk if self-custodied

**Primary value propositions:**

### 1. Monetary hedge
- Protection against fiat debasement
- Long-term store of value thesis

### 2. Treasury diversification
- Low correlation over long periods
- Asymmetric upside

### 3. Strategic positioning
- Signals innovation leadership
- Attracts capital, talent, and attention

### 4. Operational benefits
- Global settlement layer
- Treasury mobility and liquidity

## 🔐 CUSTODY OPTIONS (CRITICAL KNOWLEDGE)

### 1️⃣ Self-custody

**Pros:**
- Full control
- No counterparty risk
- True ownership

**Cons:**
- Operational complexity
- Key management responsibility

**Use cases:**
- Sovereigns
- High-conviction organizations

### 2️⃣ Institutional Custodians

**Examples:**
- Coinbase Custody
- Fidelity Digital Assets
- Anchorage

**Pros:**
- Compliance-friendly
- Insurance and reporting

**Cons:**
- Counterparty risk
- Limited sovereignty

### 3️⃣ Multi-institution / Collaborative Custody

**Models:**
- Multi-sig setups
- Distributed key holders
- Governance-driven security

**Often ideal for:**
- Corporates
- Funds
- Governments
